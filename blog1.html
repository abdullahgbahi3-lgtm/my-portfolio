How I Learned HTML & CSS

My journey into web development started with a simple curiosity about how websites are created. I began learning HTML and CSS from online tutorials and dedicated several hours every day to practicing. At first, I learned the basics such as headings, paragraphs, links, images, and forms. As my understanding grew, I moved on to advanced concepts like Flexbox, CSS Grid, animations, and responsive design.

The most important part of my learning journey was building real-world projects. I created portfolio websites, landing pages, travel booking forms, and various practice projects to strengthen my skills. Every project taught me something new and helped me improve my problem-solving abilities.

Today, I continue to learn and improve my skills every day. My goal is to become a professional Full Stack Web Developer and build modern, responsive, and user-friendly websites that provide excellent user experiences.
